🎛️ Learning Rate Effects

Learning rate is the most important hyperparameter. Too high → divergence. Too low → slow convergence.

Too High

Loss oscillates or explodes. Gradients overshoot the minimum.

Just Right

Smooth convergence. Fast but stable descent.

Too Low

Very slow convergence. May get stuck in local minima.
