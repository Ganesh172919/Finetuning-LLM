# SOTA AI — Paper Reading Guide

## Essential Papers (Must Read)

### 1. Attention Is All You Need (2017)
**Why:** Foundation of all modern AI
**Key insight:** Self-attention mechanism
**Read this:** First

### 2. BERT (2018)
**Why:** Pre-training revolution
**Key insight:** Bidirectional context
**Read this:** After transformers

### 3. GPT-2 (2019)
**Why:** Scaling laws emerge
**Key insight:** Larger models = better
**Read this:** After BERT

### 4. DDPM (2020)
**Why:** Diffusion models breakthrough
**Key insight:** Iterative denoising
**Read this:** For image generation

### 5. CLIP (2021)
**Why:** Multimodal foundation
**Key insight:** Contrastive learning
**Read this:** For multimodal

### 6. Flash Attention (2022)
**Why:** Efficiency revolution
**Key insight:** IO-aware attention
**Read this:** For optimization

### 7. LLaMA (2023)
**Why:** Open-source LLMs
**Key insight:** Efficient architecture
**Read this:** For LLMs

### 8. DPO (2023)
**Why:** Simpler alignment
**Key insight:** Direct preference optimization
**Read this:** For alignment

---

## How to Read Papers

1. **Abstract** — What's the contribution?
2. **Introduction** — What problem does it solve?
3. **Method** — How does it work?
4. **Experiments** — Does it work?
5. **Conclusion** — What's next?
